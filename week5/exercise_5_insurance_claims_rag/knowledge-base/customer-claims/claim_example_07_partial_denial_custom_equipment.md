Auto Theft Claim Example - Partial Denial of Custom Equipment
Claim ID: CLM-1007
Date of Loss: 2025-08-05
Line of Business: Personal Auto

Claim Summary
Insured reported vehicle theft from apartment garage. Vehicle recovered with missing custom audio equipment. No injuries reported.

Key Facts
- Loss location: Houston, TX.
- Police report filed and provided.
- Vehicle recovered with dash components removed.
- Claim includes 4,500 USD for custom audio equipment.

Policy and Contract References
- Coverage Verification Policy: exclusions and endorsements must be reviewed for custom equipment.
- Claim Documentation Requirements: receipts required for aftermarket equipment.

Evidence Gaps
- No receipts or invoices for custom audio equipment.
- No photos showing pre-loss installation or condition of equipment.

Denial Criteria
- Coverage excludes custom equipment without an endorsement (CP-102-DC3).
- Required documentation for claimed items is missing.

Decision
- Approved: base vehicle theft recovery costs.
- Denied: 4,500 USD for custom audio equipment due to exclusion and missing documentation.

Status
Closed - partial denial issued with coverage determination notice.
