Auto Collision Claim Example - Towing and Rental
Claim ID: CLM-1001
Date of Loss: 2025-07-14
Line of Business: Personal Auto

Claim Summary
Insured rear-ended another vehicle at low speed. Vehicle not drivable due to radiator damage. No injuries reported.

Key Facts
- Loss location: Austin, TX.
- Estimated damage: 8,200 USD.
- Police report obtained and attached.

Policy and Contract References
- Claim Intake and Triage Policy: logged within 2 hours; classified low severity.
- Claim Contact Standards: initial contact within same day.
- Towing Services Agreement: tow authorized to approved vendor.
- Rental Car Program Agreement: rental approved for 7 days at compact rate.

Actions Taken
- Coverage verified and liability accepted.
- Tow arranged to network body shop.
- Rental authorization issued with daily cap.
- Photos and repair estimate collected.

Decision
- Outcome: Approved for towing and rental under standard coverage.
- Rationale: Coverage verified, liability accepted, and required documentation received.
- Notes: Rental approved for 7 days at compact rate; towing limited to approved vendor.

Status
Open - awaiting repair completion and final invoice.
