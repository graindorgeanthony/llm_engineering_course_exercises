# Data Breach Response Claim Case Study: TechSolutions Inc.

## Overview/Purpose
This document presents a detailed case study of a cyber liability claim filed by TechSolutions Inc. following a significant data breach incident. The purpose is to illustrate the process of incident response, claim submission, assessment of covered damages, and the ultimate resolution, providing valuable insights for future claim handling and risk management.

## Scope
This case study covers the period from the initial detection of the data breach at TechSolutions Inc. on September 5, 2023, through the final settlement of the claim on November 10, 2023. It includes details regarding the incident's nature, the pre-approved incident response vendor engagement, regulatory notification processes, and the specific costs claimed and approved under the cyber liability policy.

## Definitions
*   **Data Breach:** An incident where sensitive, protected, or confidential data has been viewed, stolen, or used by an individual unauthorized to do so.
*   **Incident Response (IR):** The process of responding to and managing the aftermath of a cybersecurity incident or data breach.
*   **Personally Identifiable Information (PII):** Information that can be used to distinguish or trace an individual’s identity, such as name, social security number, date and place of birth, mother’s maiden name, or biometric records.
*   **Cyber Liability Policy:** An insurance policy designed to protect businesses from the financial impact of cyberattacks, data breaches, and other cyber-related risks.
*   **Forensic Investigation:** A detailed technical analysis to determine the cause, scope, and impact of a cyber incident.

## Incident Record: TechSolutions Inc. Data Breach

### Incident Details
*   **Claimant:** TechSolutions Inc. (Policy Number: CYB-2023-34567)
*   **Date of Discovery:** September 5, 2023, 09:30 AM PST
*   **Nature of Incident:** Unauthorized access to a customer database via a compromised employee credential (phishing attack).
*   **Affected Data:** Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and encrypted account passwords for approximately 150,000 customer accounts. No financial data (credit card numbers, bank accounts) was stored in the compromised database.
*   **Regulatory Exposure:** GDPR (for EU customers), CCPA (for California residents), various state breach notification laws.

### Initial Response & Vendor Engagement
Upon discovery, TechSolutions Inc. immediately activated its internal Incident Response Plan.
1.  **Immediate Actions:** Isolated affected systems, reset compromised credentials, and notified executive leadership.
2.  **External Expert Engagement:** Per their cyber liability policy's pre-approved vendor list, TechSolutions Inc. engaged "CyberGuard Forensics" for forensic investigation and "PrivacyLegal Counsel LLP" for legal guidance on regulatory compliance and breach notification.
3.  **Date of Engagement:** CyberGuard Forensics on September 5, 2023, 11:00 AM PST; PrivacyLegal Counsel LLP on September 6, 2023, 09:00 AM PST.

## Claim Narrative & Evidence

### Forensic Investigation Findings
CyberGuard Forensics confirmed that the breach occurred between August 28, 2023, and September 4, 2023. The attacker gained access through a successful spear-phishing attack against a junior system administrator. The attacker exfiltrated a backup of the customer database. The detailed forensic report was submitted as evidence (Exhibit A: CyberGuard Final Report, dated September 25, 2023).

### Legal & Regulatory Compliance
PrivacyLegal Counsel LLP advised on notification requirements. Given the scope (150,000 records) and data types (PII), regulatory notifications were required for GDPR, CCPA, and multiple U.S. state Attorney General offices.
*   **Notification Timeline:** Notifications to affected individuals and regulatory bodies commenced on September 28, 2023, within the 72-hour GDPR window for EU residents and 30-day window for most US states, post-determination of data scope.
*   **Communication Channel:** Email communication to affected customers with details on the incident, affected data, and protective measures, including a 12-month credit monitoring service.

### Costs Incurred (Claimed Items)
The following costs were submitted for reimbursement under the Cyber Liability Policy:

1.  **Forensic Investigation Services:**
    *   Vendor: CyberGuard Forensics
    *   Invoice Sum: \$75,000.00
    *   Description: Identification of breach vector, scope, data exfiltration analysis, system hardening recommendations. (Exhibit B: CyberGuard Invoice #2023-F-889)

2.  **Legal Consultation & Regulatory Compliance:**
    *   Vendor: PrivacyLegal Counsel LLP
    *   Invoice Sum: \$45,000.00
    *   Description: Assistance with breach notification text, regulatory filings, PII assessment, and legal advisory. (Exhibit C: PrivacyLegal Invoice #PL-2023-1015)

3.  **Customer Notification & Credit Monitoring Services:**
    *   Vendor: IdentityProtect Solutions
    *   Invoice Sum: \$30,000.00 (for 150,000 individuals @ \$0.20/person for 12-month service)
    *   Description: Mass email notification platform and provision of 12-month credit monitoring and identity theft protection for all affected customers. (Exhibit D: IdentityProtect Invoice #IPS-2023-1122)

4.  **Public Relations / Crisis Management:**
    *   Vendor: ClarityComms PR
    *   Invoice Sum: \$20,000.00
    *   Description: Development of public statements, media engagement strategy, and reputation management. Pre-approved by insurer for breaches affecting over 100,000 records. (Exhibit E: ClarityComms Invoice #CC-2023-PR-007)

5.  **Business Interruption (Operational Downtime):**
    *   Calculated Loss: \$10,000.00
    *   Description: 24 hours of operational downtime for the affected customer database and associated services during forensic investigation and remediation efforts (September 5-6, 2023). Calculation based on average daily revenue loss for the affected service line. (Exhibit F: TechSolutions Internal Revenue Report, Q3 2023)

## Decision Logic & Approval

### Policy Coverage Verification
The submitted claim was reviewed against the TechSolutions Inc. Cyber Liability Policy (CYB-2023-34567, effective January 1, 2023). The policy explicitly covers:
*   Forensic Investigation Costs
*   Legal and Regulatory Defense Costs
*   Notification and Credit Monitoring Costs
*   Public Relations Expenses (for breaches exceeding 50,000 records)
*   Business Interruption (with a 12-hour deductible)

### Claim Assessment and Approval Criteria
1.  **Documentation:** All claimed expenses were substantiated with valid vendor invoices and an official forensic report.
2.  **Necessity:** All services (forensics, legal, notification, PR) were deemed necessary for incident containment, regulatory compliance, and reputational management per industry best practices and policy terms.
3.  **Vendor Approval:** CyberGuard Forensics, PrivacyLegal Counsel LLP, IdentityProtect Solutions, and ClarityComms PR were either on the insurer's pre-approved vendor list or their engagement was explicitly approved by the claims adjuster on September 6, 2023.
4.  **Business Interruption:** The 24-hour downtime was confirmed, and the loss calculation methodology was aligned with policy terms for business interruption calculation. The 12-hour deductible applied.

### Approved Reimbursement
After review, all claimed items totaling \$180,000.00 were found to be within policy limits and coverage terms. The business interruption claim was adjusted for the 12-hour deductible.

*   Forensic Investigation: \$75,000.00 (Approved)
*   Legal Consultation: \$45,000.00 (Approved)
*   Customer Notification & Credit Monitoring: \$30,000.00 (Approved)
*   Public Relations: \$20,000.00 (Approved)
*   Business Interruption: \$5,000.00 (Approved, after 12-hour deductible)

**Total Approved Claim Amount: \$175,000.00**

## Exceptions
No exceptions were applied as all activities adhered to the policy's terms and conditions, and vendor engagements were either pre-approved or promptly authorized.

## Timelines
*   **Incident Discovery:** September 5, 2023
*   **Claim Submission:** September 26, 2023 (after forensic report completion)
*   **Claim Review & Assessment:** September 27, 2023 - October 15, 2023
*   **Final Claim Approval & Payout:** November 10, 2023

## Outcome
TechSolutions Inc. received a total reimbursement of \$175,000.00 for the covered expenses associated with the data breach incident. The timely engagement of approved vendors and thorough documentation facilitated an efficient claims process. The incident highlighted the importance of robust phishing prevention training and multi-factor authentication implementation.
