Auto Body Repair Network Agreement
Contract ID: CC-201
Effective Date: 2023-06-01
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: Alloy Auto Repair Group

Scope of Services
- Provide collision repair services for approved vehicle damage claims.
- Use OEM or insurer-approved aftermarket parts.
- Submit digital estimates and photo documentation within 48 hours.

Service Standards
- Cycle time target: 10 business days for standard repairs.
- Provide status updates every 3 business days.
- Warranty labor and workmanship for 12 months.

Pricing and Payment
- Labor rates fixed at 72 USD/hour.
- Parts priced at MSRP minus 12 percent.
- Payments issued within 15 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Maintain required licenses and garage liability insurance.
- Follow insurer safety and privacy requirements.

Termination
Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice or immediately for material breach.
