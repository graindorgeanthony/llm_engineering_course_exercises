Rental Car Program Agreement
Contract ID: CC-204
Effective Date: 2023-09-01
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: MetroRent Mobility

Scope of Services
- CC-204-S1: Provide replacement vehicles for covered auto claims.
- CC-204-S2: Offer standard, SUV, and truck classes as authorized.
- CC-204-S3: Direct billing to insurer for approved rentals.

Service Standards
- CC-204-SS1: Vehicle available within 24 hours of authorization.
- CC-204-SS2: Provide weekly utilization and cost reports.
- CC-204-SS3: Maintain fleet safety and maintenance logs.

Pricing and Payment
- CC-204-PP1: Daily rates: 45 USD standard, 60 USD SUV, 70 USD truck.
- CC-204-PP2: Taxes and fees at actual cost.
- CC-204-PP3: Payment within 20 days of invoice.

Compliance
- CC-204-C1: Customer identity verification required at pickup.
- CC-204-C2: Insurance coverage maintained for rental fleet.

Termination
- CC-204-T1: 45 days notice or immediate termination for billing abuse.
