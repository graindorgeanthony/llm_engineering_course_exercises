Catastrophe Staffing Agreement
Contract ID: CC-220
Effective Date: 2023-08-01
Term: 12 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: StormForce Staffing

Scope of Services
- Provide temporary adjusters and support staff during catastrophe events.
- Staff must be licensed in assigned jurisdictions.
- Provide daily staffing rosters and availability updates.

Service Standards
- Mobilization within 48 hours of activation.
- Minimum 40 hours per week per deployed staff member.
- Provide daily production metrics to insurer.

Pricing and Payment
- Staff hourly rates per agreed schedule.
- Travel and lodging billed at cost with receipts.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Background checks required prior to deployment.
- Follow insurer training and data security requirements.

Termination
15 days written notice or immediate termination for misconduct.
