Nurse Case Management Agreement
Contract ID: CC-208
Effective Date: 2023-02-01
Term: 12 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: CareBridge Nurse Services

Scope of Services
- Provide nurse case management for complex injury claims.
- Coordinate care plans and return-to-work strategies.
- Submit monthly progress reports.

Service Standards
- Initial contact within 3 business days of referral.
- Attend critical medical appointments when requested.
- Document care plan changes within 48 hours.

Pricing and Payment
- Hourly rate: 115 USD.
- Travel time capped at 1 hour per visit.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Nurses must hold active licenses and certifications.
- Maintain confidentiality of protected health information.

Termination
30 days written notice or immediate termination for licensing issues.
