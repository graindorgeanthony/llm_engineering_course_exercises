Property Restoration Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-205
Effective Date: 2023-05-10
Term: 36 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: RenewBuild Restoration Co.

Scope of Services
- Provide water, fire, and smoke mitigation for covered property claims.
- Perform emergency board-up and temporary repairs.
- Submit scope of work within 72 hours of inspection.

Service Standards
- Emergency response within 4 hours.
- Daily drying logs and moisture readings.
- Final photos and completion certificate required.

Pricing and Payment
- Labor rates per agreed price list.
- Materials at cost plus 10 percent.
- Payment within 20 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Maintain IICRC certifications and liability insurance.
- Follow safety and asbestos handling requirements.

Termination
30 days written notice or immediate termination for safety violations.
