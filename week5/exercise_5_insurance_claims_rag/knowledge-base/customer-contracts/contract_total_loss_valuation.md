Total Loss Valuation Agreement
Contract ID: CC-231
Effective Date: 2023-06-01
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: ValuSense Analytics

Scope of Services
- Provide total loss valuations for auto claims.
- Use market data and comparable sales within 100 miles.
- Provide valuation reports within 24 hours of assignment.

Service Standards
- Accuracy review rate below 2 percent monthly.
- Provide valuation adjustments within 2 business days of dispute.
- Maintain audit trail of data sources.

Pricing and Payment
- Per valuation: 38 USD.
- Monthly minimum: 1,200 USD.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Data sources must be documented and retained.
- Maintain security controls for VIN data.

Termination
30 days written notice or immediate termination for quality issues.
