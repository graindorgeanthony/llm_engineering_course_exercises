Salvage Auction Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-227
Effective Date: 2023-07-10
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: BidRight Salvage Auctions

Scope of Services
- Provide auction services for total loss vehicles.
- Arrange transport, storage, and listing of salvage units.
- Remit proceeds and final auction reports.

Service Standards
- List vehicles within 7 business days of pickup.
- Provide weekly auction schedules and results.
- Remit proceeds within 10 business days of sale.

Pricing and Payment
- Auction fee: 8 percent of gross sale.
- Storage rate: 25 USD per day.
- Transport billed at agreed rate card.

Compliance
- Comply with state salvage title requirements.
- Maintain buyer verification and audit logs.

Termination
30 days notice or immediate termination for compliance failures.
