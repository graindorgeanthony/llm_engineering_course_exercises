Vehicle Storage Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-214
Effective Date: 2023-09-20
Term: 12 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: SecureLot Storage Inc.

Scope of Services
- Provide secured vehicle storage for claim-related vehicles.
- Maintain 24/7 site security and access controls.
- Release vehicles only with insurer authorization.

Service Standards
- Provide intake photos within 24 hours.
- Report damage or vandalism within 2 hours of discovery.
- Maintain inventory logs with VIN tracking.

Pricing and Payment
- Daily storage rate: 28 USD.
- Administrative fee: 20 USD per intake.
- Payment within 15 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Maintain property and garage keepers liability insurance.
- Comply with local zoning and safety requirements.

Termination
30 days notice or immediate termination for security breaches.
