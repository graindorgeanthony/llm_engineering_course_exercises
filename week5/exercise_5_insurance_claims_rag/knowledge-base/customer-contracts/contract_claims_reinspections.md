Claims Reinspection Agreement
Contract ID: CC-236
Effective Date: 2023-11-01
Term: 12 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: SecondLook Inspections

Scope of Services
- Provide reinspections for disputed or complex claims.
- Verify scope of damage and prior repairs.
- Deliver reinspection reports within 5 business days.

Service Standards
- Schedule within 5 business days of assignment.
- Provide photo documentation and measurement notes.
- Maintain impartiality in findings.

Pricing and Payment
- Per reinspection: 275 USD.
- Travel surcharge for distances over 60 miles.
- Payment within 20 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Inspectors licensed where required.
- Maintain data security and confidentiality.

Termination
30 days written notice or immediate termination for quality issues.
