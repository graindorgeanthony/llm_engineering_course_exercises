Fire Investigation Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-213
Effective Date: 2023-08-20
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: OriginPoint Investigations

Scope of Services
- Provide cause and origin investigations for fire losses.
- Preserve evidence and document scene conditions.
- Deliver written reports within 15 business days.

Service Standards
- Site response within 24 hours of assignment.
- Maintain chain of custody for all evidence.
- Provide expert testimony when requested.

Pricing and Payment
- Investigation fee: 2,200 USD per loss.
- Court testimony: 250 USD per hour.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Investigators certified to NFPA 1033.
- Maintain professional liability insurance.

Termination
30 days notice or immediate termination for ethics violations.
