Legal Billing Audit Agreement
Contract ID: CC-239
Effective Date: 2023-06-05
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: CounselCost Review

Scope of Services
- Audit defense counsel invoices for accuracy and compliance.
- Identify non-billable items and rate discrepancies.
- Provide monthly audit summaries and savings reports.

Service Standards
- Audit completed within 10 business days of invoice receipt.
- Provide dispute recommendations with supporting rationale.
- Maintain confidentiality of legal materials.

Pricing and Payment
- Per invoice audited: 42 USD.
- Monthly minimum: 600 USD.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Auditors trained in legal billing guidelines.
- Secure handling of privileged materials.

Termination
60 days written notice or immediate termination for confidentiality breach.
