# Term Life Insurance Policy Contract

## 1. Overview/Purpose

This document constitutes a legally binding contract (the "Policy") between [Insurance Company Name] (the "Insurer") and the Policyholder, outlining the terms and conditions of a Term Life Insurance policy. This Policy provides a specified death benefit to the designated Beneficiary(ies) upon the death of the Insured during the policy term, provided all conditions herein are met. The purpose of this contract is to clearly define the coverage, responsibilities of all parties, premium structure, payout conditions, and other relevant stipulations.

## 2. Scope

This Policy covers the Insured for the duration of the specified policy term, under the conditions outlined in subsequent sections. It applies to individuals who have successfully completed the application process, undergone any required underwriting, and for whom the initial premium has been paid and accepted by the Insurer.

### 2.1 Inclusions
*   Payment of the death benefit upon the confirmed death of the Insured during the policy term.
*   Provisions for Policy assignment.
*   Grace periods for premium payments.
*   Reinstatement options, subject to conditions.

### 2.2 Exclusions
*   Death occurring outside the policy term.
*   Death resulting from suicide within the first two (2) years of the Policy Effective Date (or Reinstatement Date, if applicable).
*   Death resulting from acts of war, declared or undeclared, or participation in illegal activities.
*   Any misrepresentation or fraud in the application process, leading to the rescission of the Policy.

## 3. Definitions

*   **Insurer:** [Insurance Company Name], the entity providing the insurance coverage.
*   **Policyholder:** The individual or entity who owns the Policy and is responsible for premium payments.
*   **Insured:** The individual whose life is covered by this Policy.
*   **Beneficiary(ies):** The individual(s) or entity(ies) designated by the Policyholder to receive the death benefit.
*   **Policy Effective Date:** The date from which the insurance coverage begins, as stated on the Policy Declarations page.
*   **Policy Anniversary Date:** The annual date corresponding to the Policy Effective Date.
*   **Policy Term:** The fixed period of time (e.g., 10, 15, 20, 30 years) during which the Policy is in force, provided premiums are paid.
*   **Death Benefit:** The lump sum amount payable to the Beneficiary(ies) upon the death of the Insured.
*   **Premium:** The payment required from the Policyholder to keep the Policy in force.
*   **Underwriting:** The process by which the Insurer assesses the risk of insuring an applicant.
*   **Grace Period:** A specified period after the premium due date during which the Policy remains in force, even if the premium has not been paid.

## 5. Requirements and Conditions

### 5.1 Eligibility
The Insured must have been between the ages of 18 and [e.g., 65] at the time of application to be eligible for this Term Life Policy. Eligibility is subject to successful underwriting and approval by the Insurer.

### 5.2 Premium Payment Obligation
The Policyholder is solely responsible for timely premium payments. Failure to pay premiums may result in Policy lapse.

### 5.3 Incontestability Clause
This Policy, including the application, constitutes the entire contract between the parties. After this Policy has been in force for two (2) years from its Effective Date, it shall be incontestable as to the statements made in the application, except for fraud.

### 5.4 Suicide Clause
If the Insured commits suicide, whether sane or insane, within two (2) years from the Policy Effective Date, the Insurer's liability will be limited to the premiums paid, without interest. If suicide occurs after two (2) years, the full Death Benefit will be paid.

## 6. Procedures and Actions

### 6.1 Making a Claim
Upon the death of the Insured, the Beneficiary(ies) or their legal representative must promptly notify the Insurer.
1.  **Notification:** Contact [Insurance Company Name] Claims Department via phone at [Claims Phone Number] or online at [Claims Website].
2.  **Required Documentation:** Submit a certified copy of the Insured's death certificate. The Insurer may request additional documents such as the Policy contract, a claimant's statement, and proof of the claimant's identity.
3.  **Claim Review:** The Insurer will review the submitted documents and verify the claim against the Policy terms.
4.  **Payment:** Upon approval, the Death Benefit will be paid to the designated Beneficiary(ies) within [e.g., 30 days] of receiving all necessary documentation and claim approval.

### 6.2 Change of Beneficiary
The Policyholder may change the Beneficiary(ies) at any time by sending a written request to the Insurer's administrative office. The change will take effect only when recorded by the Insurer, but once recorded, it shall relate back to the date the Policyholder signed the request.

### 6.3 Policy Assignment
This Policy may be assigned by the Policyholder. An assignment will not be binding upon the Insurer until the original or a certified copy of the assignment has been filed at its administrative office. The Insurer assumes no responsibility for the validity or effect of any assignment.

### 6.4 Reinstatement
If this Policy lapses due to non-payment of premium, it may be reinstated within [e.g., 5 years] from the due date of the unpaid premium, subject to the following conditions:
1.  Submission of satisfactory evidence of insurability of the Insured.
2.  Payment of all overdue premiums with interest at a rate of [e.g., 6]% compounded annually.
3.  Payment of any indebtedness to the Insurer existing at the date of lapse.

## 7. Grace Period

A grace period of thirty-one (31) days will be allowed for the payment of each premium after the first premium. If the Insured dies during this grace period, the overdue premium will be deducted from the Death Benefit. If the premium is not paid by the end of the grace period, the Policy will lapse.

## 8. General Provisions

### 8.1 Entire Contract
This Policy, including the application (a copy of which is attached hereto), and any riders or endorsements, constitutes the entire contract between the Policyholder and the Insurer. No agent is authorized to modify this Policy, or to waive any of the Insurer's rights or requirements.

### 8.2 Misstatement of Age or Sex
If the age or sex of the Insured has been misstated, the amount payable under this Policy shall be such as the premium paid would have purchased at the correct age and sex according to the Insurer's established rates at the time of issue.

### 8.3 Free Look Period
