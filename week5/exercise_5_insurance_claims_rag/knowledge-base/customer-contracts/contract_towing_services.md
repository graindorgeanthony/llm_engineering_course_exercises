Emergency Towing Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-203
Effective Date: 2023-08-01
Term: 12 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: RapidTow LLC

Scope of Services
- CC-203-S1: Provide towing and roadside assistance for covered auto claims.
- CC-203-S2: Dispatch within 30 minutes in urban areas and 60 minutes in rural areas.
- CC-203-S3: Secure vehicles at approved storage facilities.

Service Standards
- CC-203-SS1: 24/7 availability.
- CC-203-SS2: Upload tow tickets within 24 hours of service.
- CC-203-SS3: Maintain GPS logs for response time tracking.

Pricing and Payment
- CC-203-PP1: Hook-up fee: 85 USD.
- CC-203-PP2: Mileage: 5.00 USD per mile.
- CC-203-PP3: Storage capped at 35 USD per day.

Compliance
- CC-203-C1: Drivers must be licensed and background checked.
- CC-203-C2: Vehicles must meet safety inspection requirements.

Termination
- CC-203-T1: 30 days notice or immediate termination for non-compliance.
