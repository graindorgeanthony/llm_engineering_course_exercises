Mortgagee Claims Payee Agreement
Contract ID: CC-230
Effective Date: 2023-08-05
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: Summit National Bank

Scope of Services
- Coordinate claim payments that include mortgagee interests.
- Verify mortgage status and property ownership.
- Issue joint checks and provide lienholder notifications.

Service Standards
- Verification completed within 5 business days of request.
- Payment release within 3 business days after documentation is complete.
- Maintain audit trail of mortgagee communications.

Pricing and Payment
- Per claim coordination fee: 45 USD.
- Document retrieval fee: 15 USD per request.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Follow state lienholder notice requirements.
- Protect customer financial data.

Termination
60 days written notice or immediate termination for regulatory breach.
