Claims Translation Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-219
Effective Date: 2023-09-15
Term: 12 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: LinguaBridge Services

Scope of Services
- Provide translation and interpretation services for claim communications.
- Support phone interpretation and written document translation.
- Provide certified translations when required.

Service Standards
- Phone interpretation available within 10 minutes.
- Written translations completed within 2 business days.
- Maintain translation quality score of 95 percent.

Pricing and Payment
- Phone interpretation: 2.40 USD per minute.
- Written translation: 0.18 USD per word.
- Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Maintain confidentiality and data privacy safeguards.
- Use qualified translators for medical and legal content.

Termination
30 days notice or immediate termination for confidentiality breach.
