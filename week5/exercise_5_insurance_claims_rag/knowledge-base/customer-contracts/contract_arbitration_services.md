Claims Arbitration Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-223
Effective Date: 2023-03-01
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: FairResolve Arbitration

Scope of Services
- CC-223-S1: Provide arbitration services for disputed claim settlements.
- CC-223-S2: Assign neutral arbitrators with relevant experience.
- CC-223-S3: Provide written decisions within 30 days of hearing.

Service Standards
- CC-223-SS1: Hearing scheduling within 45 days of request.
- CC-223-SS2: Maintain confidentiality of case materials.
- CC-223-SS3: Provide electronic case management portal.

Pricing and Payment
- CC-223-PP1: Filing fee: 350 USD per case.
- CC-223-PP2: Arbitrator fee: 1,200 USD per day.
- CC-223-PP3: Payment within 30 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- CC-223-C1: Arbitrators must follow applicable arbitration rules.
- CC-223-C2: Secure storage of case records.

Termination
- CC-223-T1: 60 days written notice or immediate termination for misconduct.
