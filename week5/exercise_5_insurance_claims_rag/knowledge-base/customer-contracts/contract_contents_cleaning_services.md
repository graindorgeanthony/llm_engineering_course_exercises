Contents Cleaning Services Agreement
Contract ID: CC-237
Effective Date: 2023-04-01
Term: 24 months

Parties
Insurer: Northwind Claims Services
Customer: RestoreClean Services

Scope of Services
- Provide cleaning and restoration of personal property contents.
- Offer pack-out, cleaning, and pack-back services.
- Provide inventory list and condition notes.

Service Standards
- Pack-out scheduled within 5 business days of authorization.
- Cleaning completed within 15 business days for standard jobs.
- Provide loss mitigation documentation and photos.

Pricing and Payment
- Pricing based on agreed itemized schedule.
- Storage fee: 25 USD per day for packed contents.
- Payment within 20 days of invoice approval.

Compliance
- Staff background checks required.
- Follow safety and contamination protocols.

Termination
30 days written notice or immediate termination for quality failures.
