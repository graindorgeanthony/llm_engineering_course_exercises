Claim Contact Standards
Document ID: CP-103
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Define required customer contact timeframes and documentation for claim handling.

Scope
All adjusters and claim handlers.

Policy
1. Initial contact with the insured must occur within 1 business day of assignment.
2. Contact attempts must be documented with date, time, method, and outcome.
3. If contact is unsuccessful after 3 attempts, a written notice must be sent within 2 business days.
4. Status updates must be provided at least every 14 days until claim resolution.
5. Communication must be clear, courteous, and include next steps.

Records and Retention
All contact logs retained for 7 years.
