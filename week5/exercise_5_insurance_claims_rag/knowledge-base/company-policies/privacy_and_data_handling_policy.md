Privacy and Data Handling Policy
Document ID: CP-115
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Protect customer data and ensure compliant handling of claim information.

Scope
All claim data, including personal, medical, and financial information.

Policy
1. Access to claim data is restricted to authorized staff with role-based permissions.
2. Sensitive data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.
3. Customer data may be shared with third parties only for claim handling purposes and with proper agreements.
4. Data breaches or suspected leaks must be reported within 24 hours to the Privacy Officer.
5. Paper records must be stored in locked areas and destroyed using approved methods.

Records and Retention
Data handling logs retained for 7 years.
