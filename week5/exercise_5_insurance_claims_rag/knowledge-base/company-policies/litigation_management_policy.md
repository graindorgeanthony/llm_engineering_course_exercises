Litigation Management Policy
Document ID: CP-112
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Provide guidance for managing claims that enter litigation.

Scope
All claims where suit is filed or litigation is anticipated.

Policy
1. Litigation referrals must be made within 2 business days of suit notice.
2. Approved defense counsel must be assigned based on jurisdiction and complexity.
3. Litigation budgets must be established and updated quarterly.
4. Settlement opportunities must be evaluated at key milestones.
5. All litigation activity must be documented in the claim file.

Records and Retention
Litigation files retained for 10 years.
