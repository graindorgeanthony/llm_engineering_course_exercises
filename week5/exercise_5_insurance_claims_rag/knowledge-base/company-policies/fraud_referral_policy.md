Fraud Referral Policy
Document ID: CP-105
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Provide guidance for identifying and referring suspicious claims.

Scope
All claims and claim personnel.

Policy
1. CP-105-P1: Any claim exhibiting two or more fraud indicators must be referred to the Special Investigations Unit (SIU).
2. CP-105-P2: Examples of indicators include inconsistent statements, altered documents, and delayed reporting without reasonable cause.
3. CP-105-P3: SIU referrals must be documented with supporting evidence.
4. CP-105-P4: Claims under SIU review must continue to be handled within regulatory timelines.

Decision Criteria
- CP-105-DC1: IF two or more fraud indicators are present THEN refer to SIU and document evidence.
- CP-105-DC2: IF referral is made THEN continue claim handling within regulatory timelines while SIU reviews.

Records and Retention
SIU referrals and outcomes retained for 10 years.
