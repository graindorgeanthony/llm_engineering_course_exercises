Claim Documentation Requirements
Document ID: CP-104
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Standardize required documentation to support accurate claim decisions and audit readiness.

Scope
All claim files.

Policy
1. Claim files must include: loss description, photos or evidence, estimates, and recorded statements when applicable.
2. All third-party invoices and receipts must be verified for date, vendor, and service description.
3. Any deviations from standard documentation must be approved by a Claims Supervisor.
4. Missing documents must be requested within 2 business days of identification.

Records and Retention
Documents retained for 7 years.
