Subrogation Policy
Document ID: CP-109
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Recover claim costs from responsible third parties when applicable.

Scope
All claims with potential third-party liability.

Policy
1. Subrogation potential must be assessed within 10 business days of claim intake.
2. Liability evidence must be gathered and preserved, including photos, statements, and police reports.
3. Subrogation demands must be issued within 60 days of establishing liability.
4. Recoveries must be applied first to indemnity, then expenses, then deductible refunds.

Records and Retention
Subrogation files retained for 7 years.
