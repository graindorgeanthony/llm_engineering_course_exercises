Reserving Policy
Document ID: CP-106
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Ensure claim reserves are accurate, timely, and reflective of expected loss costs.

Scope
All open claims.

Policy
1. Initial reserves must be established within 3 business days of assignment.
2. Reserves must be reviewed at each material claim event and at least every 30 days.
3. Any reserve change greater than 20 percent requires supervisor review.
4. Reserve rationale must be documented in the claim file.

Records and Retention
Reserve history retained for 7 years.
