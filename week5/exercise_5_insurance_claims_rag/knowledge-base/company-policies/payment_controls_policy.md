Payment Controls Policy
Document ID: CP-108
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Prevent unauthorized or inaccurate claim payments.

Scope
All claim payments and reimbursements.

Policy
1. Payments require two-step approval for amounts over 5,000 USD.
2. Payees must be verified against the claim file and policyholder information.
3. Duplicate payments must be flagged by the system and reviewed prior to release.
4. Payment changes after approval require documented rationale and re-approval.

Records and Retention
Payment logs retained for 7 years.
