Salvage Policy
Document ID: CP-110
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Define the handling and disposition of salvage to optimize recovery.

Scope
All claims with recoverable salvage.

Policy
1. Salvage potential must be identified within 5 business days of loss inspection.
2. Salvage items must be documented with photos and condition notes.
3. Approved salvage vendors must be used for storage and disposal.
4. Salvage proceeds must be recorded in the claim file within 3 business days of receipt.

Records and Retention
Salvage documentation retained for 7 years.
