Claim Reopen Policy
Document ID: CP-114
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Define criteria and workflow for reopening closed claims.

Scope
All closed claims.

Policy
1. Reopen requests must be reviewed within 3 business days.
2. Valid reasons include new evidence, supplemental damages, or administrative error.
3. Reopened claims must be assigned to an adjuster within 1 business day of approval.
4. A new reserve review must be completed within 5 business days.
5. Reopen approvals require supervisor sign-off.

Records and Retention
Reopen documentation retained for 7 years.
