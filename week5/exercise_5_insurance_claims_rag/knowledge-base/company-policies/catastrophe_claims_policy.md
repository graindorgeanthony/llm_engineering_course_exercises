Catastrophe Claims Policy
Document ID: CP-113
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Provide guidance for handling claims during catastrophe events.

Scope
All catastrophe-related claims.

Policy
1. CP-113-P1: Catastrophe events are declared by Claims Leadership.
2. CP-113-P2: Triage and assignment timelines may be adjusted, but regulatory requirements still apply.
3. CP-113-P3: Temporary staffing and vendor capacity may be activated within 24 hours.
4. CP-113-P4: Communication updates must be sent to impacted customers every 7 days.
5. CP-113-P5: Fast-track settlement options may be used for eligible claims.

Decision Rules
- CP-113-R1: IF Claims Leadership declares a catastrophe event THEN treat related claims as catastrophe claims for handling purposes.
- CP-113-R2: IF a claim is handled under catastrophe conditions THEN triage and assignment timelines may be adjusted BUT regulatory requirements still apply.
- CP-113-R3: IF a catastrophe event is declared THEN temporary staffing and vendor capacity may be activated within 24 hours.
- CP-113-R4: IF a customer is impacted by a declared catastrophe THEN send communication updates every 7 days until resolution.
- CP-113-R5: IF a claim meets fast-track eligibility criteria THEN a fast-track settlement option may be used.

Records and Retention
- CP-113-RR1: Catastrophe event files retained for 10 years.
