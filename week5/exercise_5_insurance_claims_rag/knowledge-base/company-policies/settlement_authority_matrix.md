Settlement Authority Matrix
Document ID: CP-107
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Define settlement authority levels to control claim payouts.

Scope
All claim settlements and payments.

Policy
1. Adjuster authority up to 10,000 USD.
2. Senior adjuster authority up to 25,000 USD.
3. Claims supervisor authority up to 75,000 USD.
4. Claims manager authority up to 150,000 USD.
5. Executive approval required for settlements above 150,000 USD.
6. Exceptions require written justification and director approval.

Records and Retention
Settlement approvals retained for 7 years.
