Vendor Management Policy
Document ID: CP-111
Effective Date: 2024-01-01
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Ensure vendors support quality, cost control, and compliance in claim services.

Scope
All external vendors including repair shops, appraisers, and restoration firms.

Policy
1. Vendors must be approved through a due diligence process including licensing and insurance verification.
2. Service level expectations must be defined in vendor contracts.
3. Vendor performance must be reviewed quarterly using cost, cycle time, and quality metrics.
4. Conflicts of interest must be disclosed and managed.

Records and Retention
Vendor evaluations retained for 7 years.
